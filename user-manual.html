1. About This System

Overview and user roles

Pallotti Presse is a web-based printing job management system. It runs in any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari) and requires an internet connection.

What it does:

  • Creates and tracks printing jobs through every production stage
  • Creates price proposals (PROFORMA) for customers
  • Records payments and generates invoices
  • Manages inventory (boutique stock and general production stock)
  • Manages employees, casual workers, payroll, and leave
  • Handles Hobe trade (batch sales with partial/overpaid tracking)
  • Generates and shares reports across roles

📋 How to navigate

5. Profile, Password & Logout

Managing your account

Your profile, password change, and logout are all accessed via the sidebar footer. Click the ⚙️ Settings button at the very bottom of the sidebar.

  • Admin role: Settings → goes to System Settings page
  • All other roles: Settings → goes to your Profile page
🔑 Change your password
1Click the ⚙️ Settings button at the bottom of the sidebar.
2Your Profile page opens. Find the password change section.
3Enter your current password, new password, and confirm the new password.
4Click Save Changes.
🚪 Logout
1Click the ⚙️ Settings button → Profile page.
2Find and click the Logout button. You are returned to the login page.
⚠️

Always log out when you finish, especially on a shared computer. Do not just close the browser tab.

17. Job Status Flow

How a job moves through the system

A job moves through these stages in order. You cannot skip stages. Each must be completed before the next begins.

Pending→ Confirmed→ In Composition→ In Montage→ In Printing→ In Binding→ In Packaging→ Quality Check→ Ready for Delivery→ Delivered→ Completed

Jobs can also be Rejected at the Pending or Confirmed stage.

Status Meaning Who advances it
Pending Created, waiting for DAF approval DAF approves → Confirmed
Confirmed Approved by DAF, waiting for production assignment Production Manager assigns → first dept stage
In Composition Design/layout department working Supervisor marks dept done → Montage
In Montage Montage assembly in progress Supervisor marks dept done → Printing
In Printing Printing in progress Supervisor marks dept done → Binding
In Binding Binding in progress Supervisor marks dept done → Packaging
In Packaging Packaging in progress Supervisor marks dept done → Quality Check
Quality Check Quality inspection being done Supervisor marks dept done → Ready for Delivery
Ready for Delivery Production complete, awaiting pickup/delivery Reception marks Delivered
Delivered Given to customer System/Admin marks Completed
Completed Job fully closed Final state
Rejected Not approved or cancelled Terminal state
🔗

The full chain: Worker clicks Complete → Supervisor clicks Mark Done → Production Manager assigns to next dept → Next dept Supervisor assigns a worker → Worker works → Repeat until Completed.

21. Troubleshooting & Quick Reference

Common problems and fast solutions

Problem Solution
Cannot log in Check email spelling and that Caps Lock is off. If still failing, ask admin to reset your password.
Page is blank or not loading Press F5 to refresh. Check your internet connection. Try closing and reopening the browser tab.
Sidebar items are missing Your role may not have that permission. Ask the Administrator to enable it in UI Permissions.
Worker sees "Employee profile not linked" Ask Admin or HR to link the worker's employee record to their login account (HR → Employees → Link button).
Supervisor sees "not assigned to department" Ask Admin to assign your user account to a department.
Supervisor "Mark Done" button is not available The worker must click Complete on their task card first. Only then does Mark Done appear.
Job not appearing for Production Manager The job must be DAF-approved first (status = Confirmed). Pending jobs do not appear in Job Planning.
Cannot create a job — no customers in list Add a customer first (type = BUSINESS) in the Customers page, then create the job.
PDF download does not work Your browser may be blocking pop-ups. Allow pop-ups for this site in your browser settings.
Data is not saving Check internet connection. Try again. If the problem persists, contact support.
Leave request shows a rejection reason Your manager provided the reason when rejecting. Read it and submit a new request if appropriate.
